Welcome! I'm a PhD candidate in Economics at Yale. I use administrative data, natural experiments, and structural models to study the impacts of government policies and transfer programs, with an emphasis on housing and education markets.

Before arriving at Yale, I studied math and economics at Davidson College. I grew up in a small town in northeastern Alabama.
