I am a PhD candidate in Economics at Yale. My research uses administrative data to study the design and impacts of government transfer and subsidy programs, with a recent emphasis on housing and education markets. I am broadly interested in topics related to redistribution and economic mobility.

I am a public, labor, and urban economist. Before arriving at Yale, I studied math and economics at Davidson College.
