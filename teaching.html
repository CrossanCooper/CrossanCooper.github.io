Teaching and Public Goods

I teach with a focus on transparent reasoning, quantitative rigor, and practical coding workflows that students can apply in policy and industry contexts.

Teaching Philosophy

I structure courses around clear causal questions, hands-on data analysis, and communication of uncertainty. Students should leave with both conceptual depth and a reproducible technical toolkit.
